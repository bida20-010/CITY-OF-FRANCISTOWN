There are places in this city that can make one wonder why they had not known about the city all along.The eyecatching Galo mall is a place of wonders that the city of Kalanga people prides itsself with.It is one of the stop magnificent european-styled supermall which offers a variety of stores,this mall has truly added an exquisite touch to the Fransistown lanscape.

THE SUPA NGWAO MUSEUM

The city's one and only meseum which was established in 1986.It preseves hundreds of untold ,mysterious stories about the city.It comprises of old pictures,incredible human made crafts,pots and other cooking utensisls from the city's inhabitants from more than a couple of decades ago.

HILL AND RUINS OF DOMBOSHABA

This an iron age stonewall site built during the era of great Zimbabwe ,it functioned as the capital of the Bakalanga tribial state.Each an every october an interesting musical event is held to celebrate the culture of Kalanga tribe in three consecutive days.
