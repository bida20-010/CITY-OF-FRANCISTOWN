Mission

As much as the city of Fransistwon prides itself with the newly western stlyed artichecture,the both the Mayor and its people are still working hard to make the city one of the saught after place of visit in Botswana.The city's council has the main mandate of installing trash tanks all around the city so as to keep it looking extra clean.Moreover,the people of neighouring villages are also working of different various ways to diversify the citys economy such as selling of arts and crafts.

Laws

The laws and rules of the city are much linked to those of the country,The city has bussineses which are required to pay the correct amount of tax especially those who qualify such as hotels ,supermarkets and shopping malls.Also every crime that takes place in the city ,those who caught in the act will be trialed first by thier customary courts only if the crime is a small offence but more complex crimes are trialed at the citys customary court.
